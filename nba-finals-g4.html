Knicks Complete Historic 29-Point Comeback to Lead Series 3-1

NEW YORK — The Knicks are never out of a game, even when making NBA history is required. On Friday night, New York completed a 29-point comeback, the largest ever in a Finals game, to take a 3-1 lead in the series.

Jalen Brunson took another step toward etching himself in New York sports lore with every thrilling bucket. But the Knicks' stifling defense set the stage for the most timely tip-in in Knicks history — OG Anunoby's soaring touch with 1.2 seconds left.

The Spurs seemed to dominate physically for most of the game. San Antonio built a lead as large as 76-49 before halftime. However, the Spurs let off the gas in the second half. By the end of the third quarter, New York trailed by only 15 points.

A 7-1 Knicks run cut San Antonio's lead to four points with 4:34 remaining. And with 1:22 left, New York took its first lead of the game.

In a postseason full of lessons for the young Spurs, this latest might prove to be the most painful. In the final two minutes, Wembanyama missed a pair of free throws, De'Aaron Fox attempted a transition layup when he probably should have run time off the clock, and the Spurs lost track of Anunoby for a winning putback.

Grammar Focus — 5 大文法考點

1
複合形容詞 數字-單數名詞
國二上 U1
▾
【數字-名詞】當形容詞修飾名詞，中間的名詞絕對不加 s，並用連字號（-）連接。
✅ a 29-point comeback   ❌ a 29-points comeback
✅ a 7-1 run   ✅ a 1.2-second tip-in
→ 考試最常考：a 14-point lead / a two-hour movie
2
the + 最高級 + ever — 有史以來最⋯
國二下 U2
▾
ever 放在最高級後面，強調「從古到今、有記錄以來」。
the largest ever in a Finals game→ 總決賽史上最大的逆轉
This is the best game ever played.→ 這是史上打得最好的一場球
3
lead / trail by + 分數
運動英文必備
▾
運動新聞最常見的兩個對立句型，考試必考。
The Spurs led by 27 points at halftime.→ 馬刺上半場一度領先 27 分
New York trailed by only 15 after Q3.→ 第三節結束時只落後 15 分
4
should have + p.p. — 當時應該但沒做
國三上 進階
▾
表示對過去行為的惋惜或批評——「那時候應該...（但沒有這樣做）」。
Fox should have run time off the clock.→ Fox 那時應該拖時間（但他沒有）
I should have studied harder.→ 我當初應該更認真讀書的
5
不規則動詞整理（本文）
國一下 U6
▾
本文出現的不規則動詞一次整理：
take → took   build → built
cut → cut   set → set   let → let
lose → lost
⚠️ cut / set / let 三態同形，最容易考！
