NBA Finals: Knicks Steal Game 1 in San Antonio

SAN ANTONIO — The New York Knicks showed everyone why they are the hottest team in the NBA Finals. On Wednesday night, Jalen Brunson scored 30 points to lead the Knicks to a 105-95 win over the San Antonio Spurs in Game 1.

The Spurs once led by 14 points in the second half, but the Knicks did not give up. Instead, New York finished the game on an amazing 11-0 run to steal the victory. This win marked New York's 12th consecutive playoff victory, making them the seventh team in NBA history to have such a long winning streak.

Victor Wembanyama, the young star of the Spurs, had 26 points and 12 rebounds in his finals debut. However, he only made 6 of his 21 shots. "I was bad tonight," Wembanyama sadly said after the game.

The Knicks are now only three wins away from their first NBA title in 53 years. Game 2 will take place on Friday in San Antonio.

Grammar Focus — 5 大文法考點

1
過去簡單式 Past Simple
國一下 U5–U6
▾
肯定句直接用過去式；否定句借助動詞 did not + 原形。
✅ Brunson scored 30 points.
✅ The Knicks did not give up.
不規則：lead→led、have→had、make→made、say→said
2
away from — 距目標還差多少
國二下 U4
▾
結構：be + 數量 + away from + 目標
The Knicks are only three wins away from their first NBA title.→ 距離冠軍只差三場勝利
3
分詞片語 , V-ing — 修飾結果
國三上 U3
▾
逗號後接 V-ing 表示前句「造成的結果」，是進階閱讀的得分關鍵。
...12th consecutive playoff victory, making them the seventh team...→ 這件事使他們成為第七支...
4
數字-名詞當形容詞（不加 s！）
國二上 U1
▾
【數字-單數名詞】修飾名詞，中間名詞絕不加 s。
✅ a 14-point lead   ❌ a 14-points lead
✅ 12th consecutive playoff victory
5
未來式 will + V — 預告下一場
國一下 U7
▾
新聞末段預告下一場，時態從過去式切回未來式，注意時態跳換。
Game 2 will take place on Friday in San Antonio.→ take place = happen（舉行、發生）
